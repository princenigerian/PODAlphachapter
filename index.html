Site History

Prince of Darkness Enterainment was founded on May 13, 2016. Founder and Cheif Information Officer Toby Nwokolo spent most his life going around North Carolina, understanding the club scene. After realizing that most of the promotion and nightlife sites catered to Charlotte, a idea was born. With Prince of Darkness, you can what it happening week in the three metropolitan cities in Norh Carolina: Raleigh, Greensboro, and Charlotte.
